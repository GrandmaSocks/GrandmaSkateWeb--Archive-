How to get Grandma Gear into your Game

To get Grandma Gear into your game you need to find your Skater XL Gear Folder

This should be in your documents folder

C:\Users\user\Documents\SkaterXL

Or a more broad example

{DRIVE}:\Users\{WINDOWS USERNAME}\Documents\SkaterXL

Then drag and drop the PNG files of the gear you downloaded!
