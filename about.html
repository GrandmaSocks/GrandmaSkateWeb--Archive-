About the brand

Grandma Skateboards is the idea of GrandmaSocks. It's a virtual skate brand stemming from the game SkaterXL. It's goal is to get SkaterXL players together with the Grandma Skateboarding Team and release decks and clothing for SkaterXL. If you are interested go to the teams page and see how you can apply. There is a good chance you will be accepted if you apply. There are no rules except have fun, interact with others, don't be toxic, and get some great clips and pictures
